Auren

"Make it here. Share it everywhere."

A custom product creation platform aimed at democratizing merchandise creation. It bridges the gap between custom product design and e-commerce fulfillment — competitive pricing, premium quality, delivered to your door.

The project started as an experiment in building a full-stack e-commerce solution and evolved into a full platform for creators to design, customize, and sell products without inventory risks.

What It Does

Auren allows users to create custom products with an easy-to-use interface. The platform handles design tools, order processing, and fulfillment logistics — taking creators from idea to delivered product without touching inventory or dealing with complex supply chains.

Development Journey

Initial Concept

Identified a gap between creators wanting custom merch and the complexity of setting up print-on-demand or managing inventory. Started building a streamlined solution.
